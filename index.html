Overview of Motion Generation.

Abstract

Human motion generation in dyadic conversations is vital for engaging Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Advances in generative modeling and multi-modal learning have enabled reaction generation from signals such as speech, conversational context and visual cues. However, generating expressive and coherent facial and bodily dynamics remains challenging due to the interplay of verbal and non-verbal cues and individual personality traits. This survey reviews body and facial motion generation, covering core concepts, representations, generative approaches, datasets and evaluation metrics. We highlight future directions to enhance the realism, coherence and expressiveness of avatars in dyadic settings.
